Welcome

We welcome you to the Temple Inn & Suites. Our hotel chain is headquartered in Bethesda, MD which builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. The owner-operated company caters to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons. Temple Inn & Suites also has accommodations for those looking for a inexpensive suite to relax from your travels. We also have accommodations for large group events at many of our hotel locations.
