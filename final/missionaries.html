Full-Time Temple Missionary Suites

Our Hotel chain has many accommodations for our full-time temple missionaries and we have a variety of suites for you to choose from.

Single Queen Bed Room

Our single bed rooms have a the most comfortable queen sized beds for anyone traveling alone or as a partnership.

Two Full Bed Room

Our two bed rooms have two options for those traveling with a co-workers or friends. For this room we have two full beds and accommodations for each person.

Two Queen Bed Room

Our two bed rooms have two options for those traveling with a co-workers or friends. For this room we have two queen beds for those traveling as couples and accommodations for each person.

Single King Bed Room

Our final room has a luxurious king sized bed for couples or anyone that needs lots of sleeping space. Our king sized rooms also have great views of any temple that you will visit.
