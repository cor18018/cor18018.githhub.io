Site Purpose

Weather Roads' purpose is to provide people of all ages and backgrounds with the ability to check weather conditions from any device at any time. People come to the site to check daily, weekly and bi-weekly weather updates. As in its name Weather Roads is a site were people can also check to road conditions of places and routes that they are wanting to go to. Weather Roads also provides live weather feeds that show road conditions in available areas as well as the levels of snow at your favorite mountain.
