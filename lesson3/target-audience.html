Target Audience

This section requires some research about the website visitors by looking into the purpose and the industry, visiting similar sites and preforming surveys to discover what scenarios are relevant to the visitor. Develop two personas to describe the target audience for the weather site. This section should describe the target audience being judicious about the selection of the most plausible demographics.

Personas

Jill is 24 has recently graduated from BYU-I and is heading back to her home in Boise. She is getting a job as a Software Engineer at a company based out of Boise. However Jill has a past with being in accidents when driving long distances and in cities so she is looking for a wesbite that will give her to road conditions to wherever she is going. She will be driving with directions from her phone so she will also be using the website on her phone.

Barry is 52 and is wanting to travel to his winter cabin with his family this coming weekend. He is looking online to see the weather conditions and what the roads are like near where his cabin is located. He uses his home desktop to use the website and browse the weather conditions as well as the conditions of the roads.

Scenarios

  1. How cold is it outside? Is it cold enough for the roads to be slick?
  2. Is it going to snow this weekend? What is the conditions of the roads right now?
  3. I need to know how hot it is going to be outside tomorrow.
  4. I need to check if I need to put on my chains today.
  5. What is the forecast for the next two weeks?
